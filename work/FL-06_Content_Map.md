## 2. The Content Map (Single-Page Portfolio)

Since the target audience is a busy CTO, a single-page scrolling layout is the most effective. No clicking around required.

* **Section 1: The Hero**
  * **Content:** The One-Line Claim in bold `Inter` font against the subtle geometric background texture. A 2-sentence intro.
  * **Call to Action:** `[View the Code]` (Anchor link that scrolls directly to the work).

* **Section 2: The Lead Case (Content Opportunity Scoring)**

  * **Call to Action:** `[View the Pipeline on GitHub]`

* **Section 3: Deep Learning / Kaggle Experience**
  * **Content:** Brief technical summaries of my work as Head of Deep Learning at Mecha ML and my Kaggle Expert status (Computer Vision, NLP).
  * **Call to Action:** `[View Kaggle Notebooks]`

* **Section 4: About & The One Action**
  * **Content:** The exact bio drafted in FL-02 ("I bridge the gap between academic research and production engineering...") alongside my real headshot.
  * **Call to Action:** `[Email Me to Build Your Next Feature]` (The primary "One Action" from Week 1).
