# FL-10: "Ship the Ugly One" - Review & Reflection

## The "Still Ugly" List
I've shipped the first iteration of the single-page portfolio. The structure and content are live, but I am well aware that these elements are currently rough and unpolished:

1. **Missing Real Images:** The placeholders for my headshot, the Kaggle Expert badge, and the DuckDB dataframe screenshots are literally just grey dashed boxes right now. I need to crop the high-res PNGs and link them.
2. **Mobile Responsiveness:** The navigation bar isn't a hamburger menu on mobile; it just squishes the links together uncomfortably.
3. **No Animations:** The page is completely static HTML. Adding subtle fade-in animations on scroll would make the case studies feel much more premium and dynamic.
4. **Hero Texture:** The subtle geometric background texture from my Identity Kit isn't applied to the CSS of the hero section yet, so it looks a bit flat.
